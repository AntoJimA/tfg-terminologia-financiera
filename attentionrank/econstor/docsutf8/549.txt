In particular, the tests employed are the automatic variance ratio test ("AVR"), the automatic portmanteau test ("AP"), the generalized spectral test ("GS"), and the time-varying autoregressive (TV-AR) approach. The empirical results validate the adaptive market hypothesis in the Vietnamese stock market. Furthermore, the results suggest that the evolution of HSX has served as an important factor of the adaptive market hypothesis.
